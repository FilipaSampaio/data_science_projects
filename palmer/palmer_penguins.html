# there is only one region with three islands

Data for these penguin species have been recorded for three islands (Biscoe, Dream and Torgensen) in the Anvers region. Ad√©lie penguins occur in all three islands, and it is the only species with data recorded in Torgensen. The data collectors found Ad√©lie and Chinstrap penguins in Dream island, and Ad√©lie and Gentoo penguins in Biscoe.

Body mass

Let‚Äôs compare body mass between the three species and see how it varies between male and female penguins (note: NA are samples for which sex was not recorded).

ggplot(penguins_raw, aes(x=species, y=body_mass_g, fill=sex)) + 
  geom_boxplot() +
    xlab("Species") + ylab("Body mass (g)")

In general for all species males tend to have larger mass (i.e., they are heavier) than female penguins, and Gentoo penguins are the larger species of the three.
